### Frequently Asked Questions
**How do I sync my health data?**
Nessa automatically asks for permission to sync with Apple Health upon first launch. You can update these permissions anytime in the iOS Settings app under Privacy > Health > Nessa.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your health data is used exclusively to generate your personalized AI insights. We never sell your data or use it for advertising.
